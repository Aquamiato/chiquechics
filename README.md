## 🖤 About

**Chique Chics** is an avant-garde luxury swimwear and editorial fashion landing page for daring women who refuse the ordinary. The site showcases micro bikinis, sculptural one-pieces, cutout swimsuits and high-fashion beachwear available through [Aquamiato.com](https://aquamiato.com).

## 🛍️ Product Categories

| Category | Styles |
|---|---|
| 👙 Swimwear | Micro bikinis, sculptural one-pieces, cutout, metallic, bold prints |
| 🎨 Editorial | Animal print, geometric, abstract, neon, tribal |
| 🧣 Beachwear | Fashion-forward cover-ups, beach dresses |
| 💋 Lingerie | Statement lingerie, fashion-forward sets |
